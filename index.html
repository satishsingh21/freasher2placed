THE COMMUNITYFARM TEAM


The communityfarm is backed by a passionate team of research personnel in the agricultural field, along with experts in technology. Our prime focus is to bring a seamless experience for the community of local farmers and farming enthusiasts – in getting closer to nature – towards a healthier lifestyle and improving the farming ecosystem together .

We at farmstop want to empower and educate farmers about the latest market trends and help them interact and engage with other farmers to share knowledge amongst themselves.
